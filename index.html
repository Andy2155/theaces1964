🌤️ The Gift of Today

“Never dwell on the tomorrow; remember, that it's God's and not ours.”
— Edward B. Pusey

We walked through halls of laughter and longing,
not knowing what the years would bring.
Yet here we are—gathered again—
not by chance, but by the quiet grace of time.

Let us rest in the present,
where memory sings and friendship blooms.
Tomorrow belongs to mystery,
but today is ours to cherish.

So we honor the now—
with music, with stories, with love that lingers.
And we trust the rest
to the hands that shaped us.
